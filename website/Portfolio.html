Junior year Writing Final

For my final assignment, I wrote an 8 page research paper evaluating the justice system in the United States. In total, I've used 10 credible primary and secondary sources to study the policies on the war on drugs and the conflict of interest they have created towards the American incarceration system. The primary source analysis section focuses on the creation of private prisons in the US. The latter half describes how modern privatization expanded to an inequality towards sentencing.

Revolutions in Iran and France: Both Sides of the Same Coin

For my integrative experience course, I aimed to compare the ideals and tactics between the 1789 French Revolution and the 1979 Iranian Revolution in 12 pages. Despite ideological differences, religion plays a major factor towards undermining the political and cultural system or Ancien Regime between the two nations.
